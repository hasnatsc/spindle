Payroll GL Mapping configure accounts for payroll journal entries

Back to Payroll
How it works: When a payroll run is Approved, the system automatically generates a PAYROLL_VOUCHER journal entry debiting salary expense accounts and crediting payable liability accounts.
Expense Accounts (Debit Side)
These accounts are DEBITED when payroll is posted (Salary Expense, Allowances).
e.g. 5101 — Basic Salary Expense
e.g. 5102 — House Rent Allowance
e.g. 5103 — Medical Allowance
e.g. 5104 — Transport Allowance
e.g. 5105 — Overtime Expense
e.g. 5106 — Other Allowances
Payable Accounts (Credit Side)
These accounts are CREDITED when payroll is posted (Salary Payable, Tax Payable, etc.).
e.g. 2201 — Salary Payable (Net take-home)
e.g. 2202 — Income Tax Payable
e.g. 2203 — Provident Fund Payable
e.g. 2204 — Other Deductions Payable (Loans, etc.)
